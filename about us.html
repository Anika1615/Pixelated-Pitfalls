Our Story

Pixelated Pitfalls started when we realized that our thumbs were getting more exercise than our legs. We were glued to our screens, scrolling through memes, watching funny videos, and trying to keep up with the latest trends. But the more we scrolled, the more we noticed how social media was messing with our heads—making us feel left out or just plain tired. We figured if it was happening to us, it was probably happening to other kids too!

So, we decided to do something about it. We wanted to create a fun and friendly place where kids could learn about social media without getting bored or feeling lectured. A place where we could laugh about the silly stuff but also talk about the serious stuff, like feeling pressured to get likes or comparing ourselves to what we see online. And that’s how Pixelated Pitfalls was born!

We’re just a bunch of 14-year-olds who get it—because we’re going through it too. Our mission is to help you scroll smarter, feel better, and have more fun both online and offline. Welcome to Pixelated Pitfalls, where we learn, laugh, and grow together!
